Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado Toast: The punch-line of a Millenial joke near you. A breakfast that defines a generation, after recoiling from the Great Recession, millenials disrupted the breakfast industry with this defining west coast-style breakfast.

Creamy. Crunchy. Green. This recipe is more than than the sum of its parts by a mile. Easily assembly makes this a simple, healthy alternative to a boring bowl of cereal.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread, whole grain
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • Pinch of salt
